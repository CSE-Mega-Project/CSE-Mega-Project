Indian Institutes of Technology

IIT Delhi

Estd. 1961

IIT Delhi is a public technical and research university located in Hauz Khas in South Delhi, Delhi, India. It is one of the oldest Indian Institutes of Technology in India.

IIT Bombay

Estd. 1958

IIT Bombay is a public technical and research university located in Powai in Mumbai, Maharashtra, India. IIT Bombay was founded in 1958. In 1961, the Parliament decreed IITs as Institutes of National Importance.

IIT Madras

Estd. 1959

IIT Madras is a public technical and research university located in Chennai, Tamil Nadu, India. As one of the Indian Institutes of Technology, it is recognised as an Institute of National Importance.

IIT Kharagpur

Estd. 1951

IIT Kharagpur is a public technical and research university established by the government of India in Kharagpur, West Bengal, India.

IIT Kanpur

Estd. 1959

IIT Kanpur is a public technical and research university located in Kanpur, Uttar Pradesh, India. It was declared to be an Institute of National Importance by the Government of India under the Institutes of Technology Act.

IIT Guwahati

Estd. 1994

IIT Guwahati is a public technical and research university established by the Government of India.

IIT Roorkee

Estd. 2001

IIT Roorkee is a public technical and research university located in Roorkee, Uttarakhand, India, headed by Director Ajit Kumar Chaturvedi.

IIT Hyderabad

Estd. 2008

IIT Hyderabad is a public technical and research university located in Sangareddy district, Telangana, India. IITH was founded in 2008, among the eight young Indian Institutes of Technology.

IIT Indore

Estd. 2009

IIT Indore is an autonomous public technical and research institute located in Indore, Madhya Pradesh, India. IIT Indore was founded in 2009.

IIT (BHU) Varanasi

Estd. 2008

IIT (BHU) Varanasi is a public technical and research university located in Varanasi, Uttar Pradesh, India.

IIT Dhanbad

Estd. 1926

IIT (Indian School of Mines) Dhanbad is a public technical and research university located in Dhanbad, India

IIT Gandhinagar

Estd. 2008

IIT Gandhinagar is a public engineering institution located in Gandhinagar, Gujarat, India. It has been declared to be an Institute of National Importance by the Government of India.

IIT Ropar
